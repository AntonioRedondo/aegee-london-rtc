Programme

Our mission is to bring aegeeans together and have fun while enjoying the attractions of London. We firmly believe in the power of non-formal education and we want to enhance the skills of our participants through involvement in workshops, talks and culture. We’ve fundraised the event exclusively with the fees of the participants. And we’re proud to present the below program:
